Emory Douglas Biography

This is a presentation of historical facts, quotes, and art from Emory Douglas, handpicked and designed to create an “accordion book” when printed out. This compilation highlights Douglas’ work from my designs of the layout, texts & fonts, and added graphics. Douglas was chosen to be the featured artist because of his historical impact; as the artist for the Black Panther Party, Douglas helped define the aesthetics of protest at the height of the Civil Rights era, cementing his status among the 20th century's most influential radical political artists. (Adobe InDesign, 2023).

What’s Meaningful to Me

This is a five-piece collection of meaningful parts of my life; family, tennis, animals, cello, and relationships. This series started off with taking photos of things that I value, then I narrowed it down to just five aspects. Next, I put each raw photo into photoshop, and brought them to life by editing the colors, textures, graphics, and captions to help emphasize the importance of each photo. Finally, I dedicated a color to each one, so that when in order, they create a rainbow while scrolling through. (Adobe Photoshop, 2023).

Arts in San Francisco Icons

This project is an Illustration of three major arts in San Francisco; the orchestra, ballet, and jazz. These three art forms are prominent aspects of San Francisco’s history, and are represented as stripped down illustrations using only outlines and primary colors. I chose these because each has a predominant role in my life; ballet represents my sister who is a ballerina, the orchestra represents myself as I have been a cello player in various orchestras, and jazz for my family who has always appreciated and respected their type of music. (dobe Illustrator, 2023).
